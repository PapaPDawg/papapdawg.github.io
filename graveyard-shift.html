Graveyard Shift

A cozy graveyard management game about daily work routines, relationships, and helping spirits move on.

Overview

Graveyard Shift is a cozy narrative management game built in Unreal Engine 5. Players work the evening shift as a cemetery caretaker, balancing routine maintenance with supernatural encounters and relationship-driven storytelling.

The project emphasizes modular gameplay systems, event-driven architecture, and emotionally paced progression. Each workday introduces new opportunities to explore the cemetery, meet lingering spirits, and gradually uncover the stories hidden among the graves.

Gameplay Loop

Start the Shift

Check in with Harold and receive your responsibilities for the evening.

Maintain the Grounds

Complete daily tasks such as removing trash, pulling weeds, and cleaning neglected graves throughout the cemetery.

Build Relationships

Meet spirits, uncover their stories, and help them find peace through conversation and repeated interactions.

Report Back

Return to Harold to review your progress, complete assignments, and collect your pay.

End the Day

Once Grandma arrives, leave the cemetery, reflect on the night's events during the drive home, and prepare for the next shift.

Systems

  • Tap / Hold / Talk interaction framework
  • Randomized daily chore generation
  • Dynamic grave state management

  • Dialogue-driven time and movement pausing
  • Multi-day progression system
  • Dynamic HUD and objective tracking
  • 12-hour in-game clock with overtime events
  • Reusable iris transition system
  • Event-driven Grandma arrival architecture
  • Gate progression and day transition framework
