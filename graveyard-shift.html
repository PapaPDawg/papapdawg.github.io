Graveyard Shift

A cozy graveyard management game about daily work routines, relationships, and helping spirits move on.

Overview

Graveyard Shift is a cozy graveyard management game built in Unreal Engine 5. The player works as a graveyard caretaker, balancing daily maintenance tasks with quiet supernatural encounters and relationship-driven storytelling.

The project focuses on building a repeatable gameplay loop where routine work, character interactions, and ghost stories gradually connect into a larger emotional narrative.

Gameplay Loop

Start the Shift

Check in with Harold and receive your responsibilities for the evening.

Maintain the Grounds

Complete daily tasks such as removing trash, pulling weeds, and cleaning neglected graves throughout the cemetery.

Build Relationships

Meet spirits, uncover their stories, and help them find peace through conversation and repeated interactions.

Report Back

Return to Harold to review your progress, complete assignments, and collect your pay.

End the Day

Leave the cemetery, reflect on what you've learned, and prepare for the next shift.
