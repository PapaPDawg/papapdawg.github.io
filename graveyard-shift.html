Graveyard Shift

A cozy graveyard management game about daily work routines, relationships, and helping spirits move on.

Overview

Graveyard Shift is an Unreal Engine 5 project focused on building a systems-driven cozy game loop. The player works as a graveyard caretaker, completes daily chores, talks with the manager, and gradually uncovers the stories of the spirits in the cemetery.

Gameplay Loop

Talk to Harold → Receive chores → Complete graveyard tasks → Return for payment → Leave through the gate → Begin the next day.
